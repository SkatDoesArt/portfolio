ABOUT ME

Hello,
My name is Zahra Salou.
I'm 18 years old and I'm a first year computer science student in Nantes.

Passionate about graphic design, digital technology, art, IT, and Formula 1,
I'm a curious and dynamic all-rounder.

Sociable, cooperative, and a bit extroverted, I enjoy teamwork while managing projects with rigor when necessary.
I'm also determined and a perfectionist, always ready to take on challenges and see things through to the end.
Although I enjoy interacting with others, I also value my private reflection time, which is essential for structuring my ideas.

Enthusiastic and tenacious, I strive to constantly improve and explore new perspectives, with an approach that is both straightforward and demanding.
My favorite work would be at the intersection of design and development, creating experiences that are not only aesthetic but also imaginative.
I'm still finding my voice, but I hope to contribute to as many projects as possible while life allows.

In the past, I had the opportunity to do a two-month training program with the BecomTech association, focusing on web development and IT.
During this training, I was able to acquire some basic information skills such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript,
which were useful for my first year of my BUT in IT.
This training also allowed me to meet CEOs and discover startups as well as large companies like ENERGIE.

In my free time, I'm usually busy drawing, cooking, exercising, spending time with my cat Cosmos,
or playing Valorant, hoping to regain my peak Elo level.
